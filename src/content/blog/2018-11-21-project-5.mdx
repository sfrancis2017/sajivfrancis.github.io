The Business Process Modeling Notation (BPMN) is a visual modeling language for business analysis applications and specifying enterprise process workflows. It is an open standard notation for graphical flowcharts used to define business process workflows. It is popular and intuitive, and can be easily understood by all business stakeholders — including business users, business analysts, software developers, and data architects.
